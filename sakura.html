SAKURA : GET A JAPANESE PALS

GENERALE IDEA ABOUT THE APP

Sakura is a chat android App based on Tinder clone mobile dating app. It is designed to provide a unique experience where members users can meet just the kind of person they want to match with whom generally are Japanese culture lovers . The infamous left or right swipe feature from Tinder is incorporated in this app which would help the users decide whether they like or dislike the person. The users would also be suggested potential Otaku Profiles to browse through to keep them engaged and to ensure that they do not lose out on a potential Friendship and exchanging language or Culture .

FEAUTURES

Email and Password Login :

This app would have an Email and Password login feature which would be used to Create a Unique Authenticating to Firebase Service. .

User Profile :

Users would be able to create their own profile by adding 4 pictures beside the profile picture and background as well , the user has to add also some optional information like his/her interests and the list of languages he/she speaks .

Find Matches :

Users can like or dislike a profile by swiping on their screen .

Settings :

Users can Edit The profile Information whenever they want so , and they can also delete the their account .

Chat :

Users can initiate a chat with the matched person after that they would be able to see the state of their matches (offline/online state) .

Browse Profile :

Users have the option of exploring other profiles, see images and check their Profile and the location on the map.

Push Notification :

Using Push Notifications you can let your users know every time a new match is found for them.
